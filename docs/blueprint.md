# **App Name**: G'Adroit Attorneys

## Core Features:

- Homepage Hero Section: A hero section with the tagline 'International Strategy. Local Authority.' and calls to action for consultation and viewing practice areas.
- Practice Area Pages: Dedicated pages for each practice area detailing services, common scenarios, and risk mitigation strategies.
- Industry Pages: Dynamic industry pages with filterable grids, showcasing expertise and relevant risk points.
- Investor Resources Hub: SEO-focused hub with guides and articles, enhancing authority and providing valuable information.
- Insights Blog: Premium blog layout with featured posts, category tabs, search functionality, and animated article cards.
- Contact Form: Conversion-focused contact page with a detailed form, confidentiality statement, and location information.
- Personalized Consultation Tool: Use a generative AI tool to analyze user input from the contact form and suggest the most relevant attorney or service, improving the speed and effectiveness of initial consultations.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep navy (#001F3F) for authority and trust.
- Background color: Light gray (#F0F2F5), a heavily desaturated navy, for a clean, readable backdrop.
- Accent color: Gold (#D4AF37) to highlight key elements and calls to action.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', a serif with an elegant, fashionable, high-end feel.
- Body font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif with a modern, machined, objective, neutral look.
- Lucide-react icons for a clean, modern look.
- Generous use of whitespace for a premium, uncluttered feel.
- Subtle fade-up and slide-in animations on scroll to reveal content.
